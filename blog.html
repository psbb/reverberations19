JAM ⚑

Just-A-Minute (or JAM) is an all round-entertaining event that is all about control of the mind over mouth. Can you make it through sixty seconds of non-stop talking without hesitation, repetition, or deviation? Or will the sheer pressure make you crumble and have your competitors pounce on you in an instant?
